How I Treat

I use a structured, analytical approach rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine to identify patterns and restore balance in the body.

Treatments may include acupuncture, herbal formulas, and lifestyle guidance tailored specifically to your condition and constitution.

My goal is not only to relieve symptoms, but to address the underlying imbalance and support long-term health.

About

Acupuncturist • TCM Herbalist • Chinese Metaphysics Enthusiast

Hi, I'm Tammy.

I began my career in accounting after earning my Commerce degree from UBC. After obtaining my CPA, I stepped away from work to raise my family.

During that time, my baby developed severe eczema. Traditional Chinese Medicine was what resolved it — and that experience changed my path completely.

I went on to study at the Alberta College of Acupuncture and TCM, and for over 10 years I’ve been serving Calgarians as a practitioner, educator, and volunteer acupuncturist.

I take a structured, analytical approach to treatment and focus on pain, migraines, and digestive health.

Outside the clinic, I enjoy time with my family, cooking, knitting, and studying foundational TCM and Chinese Metaphysics.
