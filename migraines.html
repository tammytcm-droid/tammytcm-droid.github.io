Acupuncture for Migraines in Calgary

Acupuncture can help reduce the frequency and intensity of migraines and tension headaches. Treatment focuses on identifying patterns that contribute to recurring episodes.

Rather than only addressing symptoms, care is aimed at improving overall regulation so that migraines occur less often and with reduced severity.

Each treatment is adjusted based on your presentation, with the goal of long-term stability and reduced reliance on ongoing care.
